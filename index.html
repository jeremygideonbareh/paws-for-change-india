Our Mission

Paws for Change India is a registered non-profit dedicated to ending animal suffering through rescue operations, medical care, sterilization drives, and adoption programs across Maharashtra and beyond.

Compassion First

Every animal receives individual care, dignity, and respect throughout their journey.

Medical Excellence

Full veterinary care including surgeries, vaccinations, and rehabilitation.

Community Impact

Building a culture of empathy through education and community outreach.
